About Our Farm

Farm Fresh Poultry has been family-owned and operated since 1985. Our commitment to quality and sustainable farming practices ensures that our chickens are raised in a humane environment with access to open pastures.

We take pride in providing our community with the freshest eggs and highest quality chicken products. Our hens are fed a natural diet without antibiotics or hormones, resulting in eggs and meat that are both nutritious and delicious.
